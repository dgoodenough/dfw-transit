## P2 — CUTS (data-driven)
- **Cut the routed Cleburne duplicate (E29, 0.87)** — TexPress already serves the
  corridor at 29.8. *(Resolves menu L5.)*
- **Cut Line 6** (9 km stub, 0.24, 333 commutes).
- **Cut Chico** (80 km, 0.47, 380 commutes) — keep Bowie as the single NW
  intercity leg.
- **Trim Beltline?** $22B for 6.2k commutes is the worst big-ticket item. Options:
  (a) keep full loop as 2060+, (b) keep only the south arc (Cedar Hill–Garland),
  (c) cut. **My default: (b).**

## P4 — BRT reframe
All BRT ranks ≤0.22/$M on commute OD — circumferential BRT just doesn't beat
peak driving. But the model is commute-blind to their real jobs: **feeders +
tourism**. Default: keep all BRT, phase 2055–2065, and re-justify the Stockyards
BRT (E01: 4 commutes!) explicitly as a tourism circulator. *(B1 spacing split
still applies where they're built; B2 road-snapping stays its own session.)*
